Description

Crash landing on a dangerous alien planet, you must traverse through uncharted areas in hopes of survival.

Subnautica is an open-world, survival game that follows you and your adventure through an unknown planet filled to the brim with deadly wildlife.

This game encourages the players' creativity, giving them the ability to create their own original base while also encouraging an adventurous side for the player, hiding lots of different secrets and just waiting for the player to discover them.
