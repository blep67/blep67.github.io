Description

Cuphead is a classic run and gun, plat-former game, that follows the adventures of the main characters, Cupdhead and Mugman.

Everything about this game is based off of the 1930s era of cartoons. The characters, boss fights, visual, music. Their all made with the same techniques of that era.

Traverse this strange, new world as you play as either Cuphead or Mugman, becoming more powerful and uncovering secrets.
