Description

Falling into a deep hole of the infamous mountain, Mount Ebott, you find yourself in a hidden civilisation, filled with a species called “monsters”, some friends, and some foes. Traverse this uncharted territory in hopes of reaching the surface.

Undertale is a story-rich, choice-dependent game that follows the story of a boy stuck in the “Underground”, a place where the long forgotten species, “Monsters”, reside.

Explore further into the “Underground” as you create more and more friends with “Monsters”, or follow a more deadly and cruel path to your story.
